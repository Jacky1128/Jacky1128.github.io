Short Bio
Zedong (Jacky) is currently a CSE PhD student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Dan Xu. Prior to this, he was a visiting student at Westlake University, advised by Chair Prof. Stan Z. Li (IEEE Fellow, IAPR Fellow). He also worked closely with Dr. Siyuan Li. He received his bachelor's degree in Electronics & Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), where he was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xinggang Wang.

His research centers on deep learning and efficient multi-task learning, with a specific focus on (i) efficient model architectures (MogaNet), (ii) unified visual understanding & generation frameworks (MergeVQ), and (iii) optimizers (Rep-MTL, SGG). He has been recognized as an Outstanding/Notable Reviewer at several leading conferences, including ICLR 2025, ECCV 2024, ACM MM 2024, and BMVC 2024.
