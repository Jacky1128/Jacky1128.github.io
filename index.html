Research Interests

 Currently, I mainly focus on Efficient Visual Representation Learning and AI for Genomics applications:
  • Data-level: Mixup augmentation and label-efficient learning
  • Network-level: Efficient Network Architecture Design
  • Framework-level: Vector Quantization-based Representation Learning in Computer Vision and Bioengineering
