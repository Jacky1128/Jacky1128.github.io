Discussions and long-term collaborations on related topics are welcome.

Research Interests

 Currently, I focus on Multi-modal Learning and Multi-task Learning. I am also interested in Computer Vision and AI4Science on:
  • Data-level: Mixup Augmentation and Label-Efficient Learning [OpenMixup, SemiReward].
  • Network-level: Efficient (Vision) Network Architecture Design [MogaNet].
  • Framework-level: Vector Quantized Representation Learning [VQDNA].
