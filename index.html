Research Interests

 Currently, I mainly focus on Representation Learning on Computer Vision and AI for Science (especially on Genomics):
  • Data-level: Data Mixing Augmentation and Label-Efficient Learning.
  • Network-level: Efficient Network Backbone Architecture Design.
  • Framework-level: Vector Quantized Generation and Representation Learning on Vision and Genomics.
